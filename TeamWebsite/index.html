About the Project

We are working with the team at ICEBERG. They are geologists working on categorizing the surface of Antarctica. They are currently able to look at one raster satellite image at a time but are unable to effectively view more than that. Our project is to create a map that allows them to work with the whole continent at once. We will need to create a query tool that lets them focus on curtain rock types at a time and allows for multiple types to be viewed in different colors. Not only will this allow scientists to work with a larger area but there will be an option to download the portion of the map they are currently working with. This means they will have access to this information while they are in Antarctica, where there is no internet connection. To view our capstone project proposal please click here .
